Description

The full portion of the Heart Sutra this t-shirt is referring to is:
觀自在菩薩。行深般若波羅蜜多時。照見五蘊皆空。度一切苦厄。
When the Boddhisattva Avalokitesvara was coursing in the deep prajna paramita he perceived that all five skandhas as enmpty. Thus he overcame all ills and suffering.
