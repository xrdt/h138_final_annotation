Description

The full portion of the Heart Sutra this t-shirt is referring to is:
觀自在菩薩。行深般若波羅蜜多時。照見五蘊皆空。度一切苦厄。
When the Boddhisattva Avalokitesvara was coursing in the deep prajna paramita he perceived that all five skandhas are enmpty. Thus he overcame all ills and suffering.
There are multiple ways to translate the Chinese term 行. If one subscribed to the theory of indeterminancy of translation, there is no absolute way to translate the term. The English I have presented here uses "coursing". Red Pine uses "practicing". Another uses "deeply understood". Understanding which word is most appropriate requires a deeper understanding of prajna paramita. A direct translation yields "perfect wisdom", or "the wisdom that goes beyond", or "the perfection of transcendent wisdom", as Wikipedia puts it.
